RobDroneGo Privacy Policy

Introduction:

The Porto Superior Institute of Engineering (ISEP) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal data processed within the RobDroneGo system. This policy establishes how this data is collected, processed, and protected.

Data Controller:

ISEP, the manager of RobDroneGo, is the data controller. If you have any questions related to the processing of your personal data, you can contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at the following email: [dpo@isep.ipp.pt].

Personal Data Collected:

  • Identification data (e.g., name, user ID)
  • Contact details (e.g., email, phone number)
  • Location data for task execution
  • Images captured by surveillance cameras

Purpose of Processing:

Personal data is processed with the goal of managing the operation of robots and drones, including task assignment and execution, and for security purposes within the ISEP facilities.

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Execution of a service contract for users who request tasks
  • Express consent for the processing of surveillance and location data in specific situations
  • ISEP's legitimate interest to improve security and operational efficiency

Rights of Data Subjects:

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data, as well as the right to restrict processing and the right to data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact the DPO.

Security Measures:

ISEP implements robust technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Data Retention:

Personal data is retained only for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it is processed, respecting applicable legal deadlines.

Data Transfer:

We do not transfer your personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA), ensuring that it is processed in accordance with the GDPR.

Changes to the Privacy Policy:

ISEP reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be published on this page.

Contact and Complaints:

If you have any questions or wish to file a complaint, please contact our DPO. Additionally, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the National Data Protection Commission (CNPD), the control authority in Portugal.
