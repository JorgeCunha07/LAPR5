About Us

The Porto Superior Institute of Engineering (ISEP) is a public higher education institution dedicated to the training of professionals in the engineering field. With a history spanning over 150 years, ISEP is recognized for its excellence in teaching and research, actively collaborating with the industry to foster innovation and technological development.

Our mission is to provide quality education, conduct research that positively impacts society, and contribute to technological advancement in key areas of engineering. With a highly qualified teaching staff and state-of-the-art infrastructure, we are committed to preparing our students to face global challenges in their future professional careers.

Our Project: RobDroneGo

Overview:

The RobDroneGo is an innovative project developed by ISEP, integrating robotics and drone technology to optimize processes and operations within the university campus. This system allows the autonomous execution of various tasks, from surveillance to maintenance, providing more efficient resource management and improving campus security.

Objectives:

  • Automate repetitive and high-precision tasks to increase operational efficiency.
  • Enhance campus security through autonomous patrols and continuous monitoring.
  • Provide research and development opportunities for students and faculty.
  • Reduce the ecological footprint of the campus through the use of eco-friendly unmanned vehicles.

Innovation and Technology:

We utilize the latest technology in artificial intelligence, computer vision, and control systems to ensure that our robots and drones operate safely and effectively. Our research and development team is continuously exploring new technological frontiers to improve and expand the capabilities of the RobDroneGo system.

Collaboration and Partnership:

The project involves collaboration with industrial and academic partners, allowing us to integrate practical perspectives with academic rigor. These partnerships are vital to the development of innovative solutions that can be transferred to the industrial sector.

Commitment to the Community:

We are committed to keeping the ISEP community informed and involved in the project's progress. We believe that transparency and feedback are essential for the ongoing success of RobDroneGo.
